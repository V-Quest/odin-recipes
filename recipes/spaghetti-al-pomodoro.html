Spaghetti al pomodoro

Description

When you think of Italian food the thing that first comes to mind is spaghetti al pomodoro, or spaghetti with tomato sauce. It is simple, delicious, and easy to make. The goal of a good spaghetti al pomodoro and even Italian cuisine as a whole is to let the ingredients speak for themselves. You want to use as little ingredients as possible to appreciate their flavors, this means that the quality of ingredients that you use is extremely important. Prepare this recipe 1-2 hours in advance to allow the flavors to meld together properly. Serves 4

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 16oz
  • Garlic: 5 cloves
  • Olive oil: 4 tbsp
  • San marzano tomato: 1 can
  • Roma tomato: 10 oz
  • Cherry tomato: 10oz
  • Fresh basil: to taste
  • Salt: to taste

Steps

  1. Add crushed whole garlic cloves with skin on and olive oil to a pot and heat on medium, add sliced roma and cherry tomato once oil starts to heat up. Cook for 5 minutes then add san marzano tomato and its juices from the can. Add 7-10 sprigs of fresh basil then cover and cook on low for 15-20 minutes
  2. Once the sauce has reduced add salt, remove the garlic, and crush the tomatoes with a fork or spoon. Let sauce sit for 1-2 hours before continuing
  3. Add pasta to boiling water and cook for 3-4 minutes, reserve 2-3 cups of pasta water. Finish cooking the pasta in the pot of tomato sauce and add pasta water as needed to keep the sauce moist. Consistantly stir the pasta to ensure that it is cooked evenly in the sauce.
  4. When plating add extra olive oil drizzled on top and a few sprigs of fresh basil
